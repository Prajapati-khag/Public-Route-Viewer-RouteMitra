Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

You can book bus tickets directly through our website or mobile app. Simply select your origin, destination, travel date, and number of passengers. Then choose from available buses and complete the payment process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and popular mobile wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.

How can I track my bus in real-time?

After booking, you'll receive a confirmation SMS/email with a link to track your bus. You can also track it directly on our website or app by entering your PNR number.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure receive a 90% refund. Between 12-24 hours before departure, a 50% refund is provided. Cancellations within 12 hours of departure are non-refundable.

How do I provide feedback about a recent journey?

You can use the form on this page to submit your feedback. Alternatively, you can email us at feedback@routemitra.com or call our customer support number.
